12 """Work around a recurring segfault in conda's libfontconfig 

13 (FcCharSetFindLeafForward): the env shares ~/.cache/fontconfig with the 

14 system's different-version fontconfig and crashes reading an incompatible 

15 cache. Point fontconfig at a private cache dir (keeping every font dir), so 

16 it only ever reads its own cache. Must run before Qt loads its font system. 

17 

18 Linux + conda only; skipped if FONTCONFIG_FILE or COZER_NO_FONT_FIX is set. 

19 Best-effort: any failure leaves fontconfig untouched. 

20 """
